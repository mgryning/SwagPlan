How to Delete Your Data

If you would like to delete your personal data from SwagPlan, please follow the instructions below:

Data We Store

  • Your Facebook name and ID
  • Your email address (if provided)
  • Activity participation history
  • Activities you've created or been responsible for

To Request Data Deletion

Email us at:

mortengryning@gmail.com

Subject: SwagPlan Data Deletion Request

What to Include in Your Request

  • Your full name as it appears in SwagPlan
  • The email address associated with your Facebook account
  • Any additional information to help us identify your account

What Happens Next

  1. We will verify your identity to ensure the security of your data
  2. Your personal information will be permanently deleted from our systems
  3. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete
  4. Processing typically takes 1-3 business days

Important Notes

  • Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered
  • Activities you created may remain for other users, but your name will be removed
  • You can create a new account at any time by logging in again

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion or our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to contact us at mortengryning@gmail.com.
